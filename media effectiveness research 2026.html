Top Performers: Awareness

\
\

Primary Driver: Video (TV & OLV). The combination of sight, sound, and motion remains the most effective way to establish memory structures. Broad reach allows for low CPMs at scale.

\

The Challenger: Audio. Neuroscience data indicates that audio ads (specifically in podcasts) generate higher emotional intensity than video. While video captures the eye, audio captures the imagination ("Theater of the Mind").

\
    \
  • TV/Video: 92% Reach potential. Highest cost per entry.
    • \
  • Audio: High "Attention" metric. Less skip-prone than YouTube.
    • \
  • Display: Weakest for awareness; best used for frequency maintenance.

\ Deep Dive\

The Audio Renaissance

\

Why Audio (Podcasts & Streaming) is outperforming expectations in modern media mixes.

\
\ \
\ \
\

The Intimacy Effect

\

Podcast listeners describe hosts as "friends." This para-social relationship drives a trust score significantly higher than influencers on Instagram/TikTok.

\
\ \
\

Unskippable Attention

\

Unlike social feeds where users scroll past ads, audio is often consumed while hands are busy (driving, cooking), leading to higher ad completion rates (90%+).

\
\ \
\

Contextual Relevance

\

Targeting by genre (e.g., True Crime, Business) ensures high contextual alignment, which boosts purchase intent by +14% compared to run-of-network buys.

Strategic Recommendations

\ \
\
\
\ \
\
\

Diversify beyond "Sight"

\

Brands over-invest in visual channels (Display, Social). Shift 10-15% of budget to Audio to capture "eyes-free" attention moments and capitalize on lower CPMs.

\
\
\ \
\
\ \
\
\

Leverage the "Full Funnel" Audio Strategy

\

Use Streaming Music ads for reach/frequency (Awareness) and Host-Read Podcast ads for depth/trust (Consideration). Do not treat them as the same channel.

\
\
\ \
\
\ \
\
\

Measure Correctly

\

Last-click attribution kills brand growth. Incorporate MMM or Incremental Lift studies to see the true value of Awareness drivers like TV and Audio.
